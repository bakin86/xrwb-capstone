Our Story


Founded in 2005 in Wichita, Kansas, Best Cars Dealership began as a small family-owned lot with a simple mission: connect people with cars they love at prices that make sense.

Over nearly two decades we've grown to seven locations across five states – but our core values haven't changed. Every salesperson, mechanic, and finance specialist on our team shares the same commitment to honesty, quality, and customer-first service.

Whether you're buying your first car, upgrading your family SUV, or adding a work pickup to your fleet, our experts guide you through every step — from browsing inventory to driving off the lot.
